## Getting Started

### Creating an Account

1. **Visit MarketBridge** at your deployment URL
2. **Click "Sign Up"** in the top right corner
3. **Choose your signup method:**
   - **Google** - Quick signup with your Google account
   - **Email & Password** - Traditional signup
   - **Facebook** - Sign up with Facebook (if configured)

4. **Complete your profile:**
   - Enter your display name
   - Add your location (optional)
   - Choose your role:
     - **Customer** - Buy products
     - **Dealer** - Sell products

### Logging In

1. Click **"Login"** in the top right
2. Enter your credentials or use Google/Facebook
3. You'll be redirected to the homepage

## For Customers

### Browsing Products

1. **Navigate to "Listings"** from the menu
2. **Use filters:**
   - Search by product name
   - Filter by category
   - Browse all listings

3. **View product details:**
   - Click on any product card
   - See images, price, description
   - View dealer information

### Buying a Product

#### Step 1: Contact the Dealer
1. Click **"Contact Dealer"** on the product page
2. A chat will open (or create a new one)
3. Discuss product details, ask questions

#### Step 2: Initiate Escrow Payment
1. In the chat, click **"Initiate Escrow"**
2. Enter:
   - **Amount** - Agreed price
   - **Shipping Address** - Your delivery address
   - **Phone Number** - Contact number
3. Click **"Initiate Escrow"**

#### Step 3: Wait for Shipping
1. Dealer will update order status to "Shipped"
2. You'll receive a notification in chat
3. Track your order in **"Orders"** page

#### Step 4: Confirm Delivery
1. Receive your product
2. Go to **"Orders"** page
3. Click **"Confirm Delivery"**
4. Payment is released to the dealer

### Managing Orders

1. Click **"Orders"** in the menu
2. View all your purchases
3. See order status:
   - **Pending** - Payment held in escrow
   - **Shipped** - Product on the way
   - **Delivered** - Order complete

## For Dealers

### Setting Up Your Business

1. **Sign up as a Dealer**
2. **Complete your profile:**
   - Business name
   - Store type (Physical/Online/Both)
   - Location

### Creating a Listing

1. **Go to Dealer Dashboard**
2. Click **"Add New Listing"**
3. **Fill in details:**
   - Title
   - Description
   - Price (₦)
   - Category
   - Location
   - Product images (URLs)
4. Click **"Create Listing"**

### Managing Listings

1. **View all listings** in "My Listings"
2. **Edit** - Update product details
3. **Deactivate** - Hide from customers
4. **Delete** - Remove permanently

### Managing Orders

1. **Go to Dealer Dashboard**
2. **View all orders:**
   - Pending - New orders
   - Shipped - Orders you've shipped
   - Completed - Delivered orders

3. **Update order status:**
   - Select order
   - Choose "Mark as Shipped"
   - Customer will be notified

### Receiving Payments

1. Customer initiates escrow
2. You receive notification in chat
3. Ship the product
4. Update status to "Shipped"
5. Customer confirms delivery
6. **Payment released to you!**

## Escrow System

### How It Works

```
Customer → Initiates Escrow → Funds Held Securely
                                      ↓
Dealer → Ships Product → Updates Status
                                      ↓
Customer → Receives Product → Confirms Delivery
                                      ↓
                            Funds Released to Dealer
```

### Benefits

✅ **Buyer Protection** - Funds held until delivery confirmed  
✅ **Seller Protection** - Payment guaranteed after shipping  
✅ **Dispute Resolution** - Secure transaction history  
✅ **Transparency** - All parties see order status  

### Safety Tips

- ✅ Always use escrow for payments
- ✅ Communicate through MarketBridge chat
- ✅ Verify product before confirming delivery
- ✅ Keep chat history as proof
- ❌ Never pay outside the platform
- ❌ Don't share personal payment details

## FAQ

### General

**Q: Is MarketBridge free to use?**  
A: Yes! Browsing and buying is completely free.

**Q: How do I know if a dealer is verified?**  
A: Look for the "Verified" badge on listings.

**Q: Can I sell on MarketBridge?**  
A: Yes! Sign up as a dealer and start listing products.

### Payments

**Q: How does escrow work?**  
A: Your payment is held securely until you confirm delivery. Only then is it released to the seller.

**Q: What if I don't receive my product?**  
A: Don't confirm delivery. Contact support through chat.

**Q: When do I get paid as a dealer?**  
A: After the customer confirms delivery.

### Orders

**Q: How do I track my order?**  
A: Go to "Orders" page to see all your purchases and their status.

**Q: Can I cancel an order?**  
A: Contact the dealer through chat to discuss cancellation.

**Q: What if the product is damaged?**  
A: Don't confirm delivery. Contact the dealer immediately.

### Technical

**Q: Why can't I see my messages?**  
A: Make sure you're logged in. Refresh the page if needed.

**Q: My order status isn't updating**  
A: The page updates in real-time, but you can refresh manually.

**Q: I forgot my password**  
A: Use "Forgot Password" on the login page (if using email signup).
